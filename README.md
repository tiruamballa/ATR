# Overview

ATR (Adaptive Tracking & Roadmap) is a personal career execution platform designed for B.Tech students preparing for internships, placements, and long-term software engineering careers.

Unlike traditional roadmap websites that only display learning paths, ATR focuses on execution, accountability, progress tracking, attendance management, and career readiness.

The platform transforms large career goals into daily actionable tasks and continuously tracks progress across technical skills, DSA, aptitude, interview preparation, and college attendance.

* Daily attendance entry

* Schedule management
* Manual attendance corrections

---

## Attendance Formula

Attendance %

=

Present Periods ÷ Total Periods × 100

---

## Attendance Status

### SAFE

Attendance ≥ 76%

---

### WARNING

Attendance 70% – 75.99%

---

### DANGER

Attendance < 70%

---

## Buffer Calculator
